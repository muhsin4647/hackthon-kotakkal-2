Traffic Rules Awareness

Promoting safe driving practices in Kottakkal

Helmet Safety

Always wear a helmet when riding a two-wheeler

Seat Belt Usage

Buckle up for every trip, no matter how short

No Drunk Driving

Never drive under the influence of alcohol

Speed Limits

Adhere to posted speed limits for everyone's safety

Pedestrian Crossings

Always yield to pedestrians at crosswalks

No Mobile Use

Avoid using mobile phones while driving

Share Your Safe Driving Photo

Help promote traffic safety awareness by sharing photos of you following traffic rules

Why Following Traffic Rules Matters

  • Reduces accidents and saves lives
  • Ensures smooth traffic flow in Kottakkal
  • Protects pedestrians, especially near schools and hospitals
  • Prevents traffic fines and penalties
  • Sets a good example for others in the community
  • Helps emergency vehicles reach their destinations faster
